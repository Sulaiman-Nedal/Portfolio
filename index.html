Projects

b-it Project

Maze Navigation with MLM-U Multistep Prediction

Implements a transformer model trained with a Multi-Step Look-Ahead (MLM-U) objective to navigate mazes. Achieves higher accuracy and faster convergence in maze solving compared to standard next-token prediction, even outperforming much larger models and those using A* supervision. Includes full training and evaluation setup from the "Transformers Can Navigate Mazes With Multi-Step Prediction" paper by Facebook Research.

Bonn-Uni Project

Biomedical Relation Extraction with BioBERT, SciFive & QA4RE

Hands-on NLP lab exploring biomedical relation extraction on the BioRED dataset using BioBERT and SciFive, including data preprocessing/tokenization, model training, and evaluation. Also implements a QA-style framing (QA4RE) for relation extraction.

Bonn-Uni Project

Telemetry-Based Churn Prediction in Mobile Racing Games

End-to-end ML pipeline that engineers gameplay features from two mobile games, builds Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, and Random Forest models, and evaluates churn within a 10-day horizon after a 5-day observation window. Includes config-driven runs, saved features/models/metrics, and feature-importance analysis following Kim et al. (2017).
